We work with companies, sponsors, freelancers & developers to organise events. We also give organisers an opputunity to customize their events.

TIMELINE

2021

AR, VR & RFID

Started using AR, VR and RFID in events to give everyone a better experience. RFID helps in accelerating online registration and onsite check-in process, elevate the data analyses to develop a more targeted event marketing strategy.

2020

Machine Learning

We implement ML(Machine Learning) on our website to help people get a complete plan for their event by analysing data of previous events. The plan can be customized by later on by the organizer.

2019

Online Events

Organised our first online event in March,2019. Then we started growing as we could easily organise online events around the country sitting at a place. Also got an oppurtunity to host one of the most famous concerte in India.

2018

Established

OnE Touch was founded by Shashwat, Anurag and Arif. A event management company to help people organise events easily.
